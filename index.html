What you can create

Three creative tools, powered by state-of-the-art generative AI models.

🎬

Motion Control

Animate a character photo with the movement from any short reference video — your image comes to life following the motion you choose.

👶

Future Baby

Upload two parent photos and see a realistic AI-generated image of what your future child could look like. Made for expecting moms dreaming about the little one on the way.

✨

Trends

Recreate the latest viral animation trends from a single photo, ready to share as short-form video content. Built for bloggers and young creators who post AI content across their social media.

How it works

STEP 1

Upload

Pick a photo — and, for Motion Control, a short reference video (3–30 seconds).

STEP 2

Generate

Our AI processes your input securely in the cloud and creates your result in minutes.

STEP 3

Save & share

Download your video or image and share it anywhere.
